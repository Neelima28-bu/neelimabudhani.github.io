About Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence involves creating systems that mimic human intelligence. It enhances classroom engagement, personalizes learning, and supports decision-making for educators.

Objectives

  • Understand AI concepts and their educational relevance.
  • Train educators in AI-powered teaching strategies.
  • Explore AI in academic administration and research.
  • Provide hands-on experience with AI tools.
  • Build a network of AI-enabled educators.
